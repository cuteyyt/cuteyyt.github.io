I am a PhD student at the [College of Computing and Data Science](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/computing), Nanyang Technological University (NTU, Singapore), supervised by [Prof. Lin Guosheng](https://guosheng.github.io/). I am currently also a Research Intern at Tencent AIPD.

I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/) in 2022, where I worked closely with [Prof. Song Mingli](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=7oLbhAwAAAAJ) and [Prof. Shen Chengchao](https://chengchaoshen.github.io/).

## Research Interests

My research focuses on 3D editing and reconstruction, with a broader interest in generative approaches for 3D content.
